Hotel Policies

Please review our policies to ensure a comfortable stay for all guests.

Check-in / Check-out

  • Check-in: 2:00 PM
  • Check-out: 11:00 AM
  • Early check-in and late check-out available upon request (subject to availability).

Cancellation Policy

Free cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in. Cancellations made within 48 hours will be charged the first night's rate.

Children & Extra Beds

Children of all ages are welcome. Complimentary cribs available for infants. Extra beds available for $30/night.

Pets

We are a pet-friendly resort! Small pets (up to 10kg) are allowed with a $50 cleaning fee. Please notify us in advance.
